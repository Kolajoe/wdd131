Welcome to Green Energy DRC

Overview of DRC energy challenges & opportunities. The Democratic Republic of Congo holds some of Africa's most abundant renewable energy resources. From the mighty Congo River to year-round tropical sunshine, the DRC has the potential to become a green energy leader and power millions of homes across Central Africa.
